Les résultats de la semaine passée

Le oui

I throw myself down among the tall grass by the trickling stream; and, as I lie close to the earth, a thousand unknown plants are noticed by me: when I hear the buzz of the little world among the stalks.

Le non

Grow familiar with the countless indescribable forms of the insects and flies, then I feel the presence of the Almighty, who formed us in his own image, and the breath of that universal love which bears and sustains us.

Les sources pertinentes

  • Absorbed - in the exquisite sense of mere tranquil existence, that I neglect my talents. I should be incapable of drawing a single stroke.
  • Moment - yet I feel that I never was a greater artist than now. When, while the lovely valley teems with vapour around me.
  • Sun - strikes the upper surface of the impenetrable foliage of my trees, and but a few stray gleams steal into the inner sanctuary.
